# Design Proposal: Jorge Flavio Sarmiento Falla

## Identidad
Jorge Flavio is a Peruvian-born industrial engineer who built his entire academic career at UCF — from BS to PhD in 10 years. He bridges the gap between engineering systems and healthcare humanity, researching how technology and HR practices can make hospital workforces more agile. An award-winning teacher of 1000+ students who turns complex systems thinking into accessible knowledge.

## Audiencia
- **Academic peers & research collaborators** → publications, research interests, Google Scholar
- **Universities seeking faculty** → teaching credentials, awards, student evaluations
- **Graduate students** → courses taught, mentoring approach
- **Industry professionals** → Lean Six Sigma, simulation expertise

## Metáfora Visual
The precision and clarity of engineering blueprints meets the warmth of academic mentorship. Clean lines, structured grids, generous whitespace — like a well-organized research paper brought to life. UCF-inspired cool blue palette signals institutional credibility while maintaining a personal, approachable feel.

## Paleta (6 colores) — Monocromático frío (azul)
- **primaryDark:** `#0C2D48` — Deep navy. Headings, footer, nav. Authority + academia.
- **primary:** `#2E86AB` — UCF-inspired medium blue. Borders, badges, section accents.
- **primaryLight:** `#A3D5FF` — Light sky blue. Shimmer highlights, subtle accents.
- **accent:** `#1B6B93` — Strong cerulean. CTAs, stats numbers, active states. Pops against white.
- **surface:** `#F0F6FA` — Ice blue-grey. Section backgrounds. Derived from primary, desaturated.
- **surfaceLight:** `#F8FBFD` — Near-white with blue undertone. Hero bg, lighter sections.

## Tipografía
- **Headings:** Inter (700/800) — Modern, precise, engineering-clean
- **Body:** Source Sans 3 (400/600) — Readable, academic-friendly
- **Accent/Numbers:** Space Grotesk (500) — For stats, publication counts, metrics

## Efecto Visual Único
**"Research Impact Visualization"** — Publication cards with animated citation counter on hover, journal badge pills (Applied Sciences, Int'l J. of Industrial Ergonomics), and a connected-dots research network graphic showing research themes linking to each other.

**"Academic Fingerprint"** — Visual row of academic profile icons (Google Scholar, ResearchGate, ORCID, UCF) with subtle pulse animation, showing his distributed academic presence.

## Secciones que NO incluir
- **Services** — He's a university lecturer, not selling services
- **CTA "Hire Me"** — Inappropriate for academic context
- **Projects** (generic) — Replaced by Publications (more relevant)
- **Testimonials** — No client testimonials, academic context

## Mobile Strategy (375px)
- Hero: stack photo above text
- Stats: horizontal scroll or 3-col tight
- Publications: full-width stacked cards
- Timeline: single column, dots on left
- Education: full-width stacked
- Skills: pill wrap
